Missoula Spirit &
Ale Trail

  • Company: Geoff Pavey Design
  • Role: Design, Developement
  • Platform(s): Web, Print

The Missoula Spirit & Ale Trail is a self-guided tasting tour through Missoula Montana. It includes 9 unique craft breweries and 3 distilleries. I did the complete site design, branding, printwork and site development. It was a big project with many layers but I had a great time doing both traditional print design and marrying that with an internet presense.
